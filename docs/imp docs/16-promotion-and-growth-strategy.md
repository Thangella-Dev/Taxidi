# 16 - Taxiro Promotion And Growth Strategy

**Date:** 23 July 2026

## Promotion Goal

Taxiro should not spend heavily on broad ads in the beginning. It should first build density in one local area, then expand.

## Best Launch Channels

| Channel | Why it works | Cost level |
|---|---|---|
| College/hostel ambassadors | High local density and repeat rides | Low-medium |
| Apartment/PG partnerships | Daily commute demand | Low-medium |
| Office/corporate tie-ups | Repeat predictable demand | Medium |
| Rider referral program | Supply growth | Medium |
| User referral program | Low-cost acquisition | Medium |
| WhatsApp communities | Local trust and low cost | Low |
| Instagram local ads | Awareness | Medium |
| Google search ads | Capture urgent intent | Medium-high |
| Offline posters/QR stands | Local discovery | Low-medium |
| Local events/campus booths | Trust building | Medium |

## Recommended First Promotion Plan

### Month 1 - Community And Supply

- Recruit 30-50 riders.
- Visit PGs, hostels, local shops, colleges.
- Create QR posters.
- Give first 500 users invite/referral access.
- Give riders completion bonuses for first 100 rides.

Budget: **Rs 50,000-Rs 1,50,000**

### Month 2 - Demand Activation

- Launch user referral: give Rs 20-Rs 40 ride credit.
- Launch rider referral: give Rs 100-Rs 300 after referred rider completes rides.
- Run Instagram ads within exact launch area.
- Partner with local cafes/gyms/coaching centers.

Budget: **Rs 1,00,000-Rs 3,00,000**

### Month 3 - Local Brand Push

- Increase ads only in zones with enough rider supply.
- Add corporate/campus commute tie-ups.
- Add branded rider jackets/stickers if legally and operationally suitable.
- Add safety campaign: verified riders, ride code, SOS.

Budget: **Rs 2,00,000-Rs 6,00,000**

## Promotion Offers

Use carefully:

- First ride discount capped at Rs 30-Rs 50.
- Referral credit after completed ride, not signup.
- Peak-hour rider bonus only when supply shortage exists.
- No unlimited coupons.
- No fake GMV growth that destroys margins.

## Best Message For Users

- Fast local rides.
- Verified riders.
- Private ride code.
- Live tracking.
- Fair price.
- Easy support.

## Best Message For Riders

- More local rides.
- Transparent commission.
- Fast payment/settlement.
- Flexible online/offline.
- Verified customer flow.
- Less idle time through demand signals.

## Key Growth Metrics

- Cost per acquired user.
- Cost per completed first ride.
- Repeat ride rate within 7 days.
- Rider online hours.
- Rider earnings per hour.
- Coupon cost per completed ride.
- Cancellation rate.
- Average pickup time.
